1. Purpose

The Recruiter Portal provides Recruiters with the tools required to manage their company, create job opportunities, review Candidates, manage hiring pipelines, schedule interviews, and track recruitment performance.

2. Recruiter Portal Structure
Recruiter Portal
│
├── Dashboard
├── Jobs
├── Applicants
├── Hiring Pipeline
├── Interviews
├── Company
├── Analytics
└── Settings
3. Recruiter Access

Recruiter Portal access requires:

Authenticated
+
Role = Recruiter
+
Account Status = Active
+
Verification Status = Approved

Only approved Recruiters can access protected Recruiter Portal features.

Recruiters can manage their Company's job openings.

Recruiters can:

Create Job

Publish Job

View Job

Job lifecycle:

Draft
    ↓
Pending Review
    ↓
Published
    ↓
Paused
    ↓
Closed

The exact job approval process should follow Career77's job moderation and verification rules.

Where messaging is enabled, Recruiters can communicate with Candidates involved in their recruitment process.

Possible actions:

Send Message
Receive Message

16. Recruiter Quick Actions

The Dashboard may provide quick actions:

Create Job

These actions should provide quick access to common recruitment tasks.

17. Recruiter Data Access

Recruiters must only access data they are authorized to manage.

Example:

Recruiter A
    ↓
Company A
    ↓
Company A Jobs
    ↓
Company A Applicants

Recruiter A must not access:

Company B
Company B Jobs
Company B Applicants

Backend authorization is mandatory.

18. Recruiter Security

The Recruiter Portal must:

Require authentication.
Verify Recruiter role.
Verify account status.
Verify Super Admin approval.
Enforce backend authorization.
Protect Candidate information.
Protect Company information.
Protect resumes and documents.
Secure interview information.
Record important recruitment activities.

22. Recruiter Portal Loading States

All major Recruiter pages should support loading states.

Loading
    ↓
Skeleton UI
    ↓
Data Loaded

Loading states should prevent unnecessary layout shifts.

23. Recruiter Portal Error States

If data cannot be loaded:

Unable to load the requested information.

Please try again.

Action:

[ Retry ]

Internal server, database, or security errors must not be exposed to Recruiters.

24. Recruiter Portal Acceptance Criteria

The Recruiter Portal is complete when:

Approved Recruiters can access the Portal.
Recruiters can view the Dashboard.
Recruiters can manage Jobs.
Recruiters can view Applicants.
Recruiters can manage the Hiring Pipeline.
Recruiters can manage Interviews.
Recruiters can manage Company information.
Recruiters can view Analytics.
Recruiters can manage Settings.
Recruiters receive relevant Notifications.
Recruiter data access is restricted.
Candidate data is protected.
Backend authorization is enforced.
Empty states are handled.
Loading states are handled.
Error states are handled.
Portal is responsive.
Portal is accessible.
