1. Purpose

The Company feature allows Recruiters to view and manage their Company Profile.

Recruiters can:

View Company Profile
Edit Company Information
Upload Company Logo
Add Company Description
Manage Company Contact Details
Manage Company Location
Add Company Website
Add Social Links
View Company Verification Status
2. Company Access

Route:

/recruiter/company

Access requires:

Authenticated
+
Role = Recruiter
+
Account Status = Active
+
Verification Status = Approved
3. Company Flow
Recruiter
    ↓

    ↓

20. Open Jobs

The Company Profile may display active Job Openings.

Example:

Open Positions

Full Stack Developer
Frontend Developer
Backend Developer

Candidates can select a Job to view its details.

21. Company Recruiter Access

If Career77 supports multiple Recruiters under one Company, authorized Recruiters may access Company information.

Example:

Company
    │
    ├── Recruiter A
    ├── Recruiter B
    └── Recruiter C

Access should be controlled using Company membership and authorization rules.

22. Company Ownership

The system must ensure that Recruiters can only manage Companies they are authorized to manage.

Recruiter
    ↓
Company Membership
    ↓
Company Authorization
    ↓
Company Profile Access

Recruiters must not modify another Company's information.

27. Validation

Company information should be validated before saving.

Examples:

Company Name is required.
Industry is required.
Website URL is invalid.
Email address is invalid.

The Recruiter should be able to correct validation errors.

        ↓

        ↓
