1. Purpose

The Recruiter Jobs feature allows Recruiters to create and manage job openings for their Company.

Recruiters can:

View Jobs
Create Jobs
Edit Jobs
Publish Jobs
Pause Jobs
Close Jobs
Manage Job Status
View Applicants
2. Jobs Access

Route:

/recruiter/jobs

Access requires:

Authenticated
+
Role = Recruiter
+
Account Status = Active
+
Verification Status = Approved
3. Jobs Flow
Recruiter
    ↓
Jobs
    ↓
Create Job
    ↓

    ↓
Save Draft
    ↓
Review Job
    ↓
Publish Job
    ↓
Candidates View Job
    ↓
Candidates Apply
    ↓
Recruiter Reviews Applicants

The Job Description may include:

Role Overview
Responsibilities

Preferred Skills

9. Employment Type

Supported options may include:

Part Time

Internship

The available options should follow Career77's job configuration.

Recruiters can specify the required experience level.

Fresher
Junior
Mid-Level
Senior
Lead

Recruiters may also specify an experience range.

Example:

Minimum Experience: 2 Years
Maximum Experience: 5 Years
11. Work Mode

Recruiters can select:

On-site
Hybrid
Remote

If the Job is location-specific, the Recruiter should provide the relevant location.

Minimum Salary
Maximum Salary
Salary Period
Currency

₹6 LPA – ₹10 LPA

14. Save as Draft

Recruiters can save incomplete Jobs.

Create Job
    ↓
Enter Details
    ↓
Save Draft

Draft Jobs are not visible to Candidates.

Example:

Job Status: Draft

Recruiters can return later and continue editing.

15. Job Review

Before publishing, Recruiters should review the Job information.

Draft
    ↓
Review
    ↓
Confirm Details
    ↓
Publish

The system should display all important Job information before final submission.

16. Publish Job

After completing the Job:

Draft
    ↓
Publish
    ↓
Validation
    ↓
Published

If Career77 requires Super Admin moderation:

Draft
    ↓
Submit for Review
    ↓
Super Admin Review
    ↓
Approved
    ↓
Published
17. Published Job

A Published Job becomes available to Candidates according to Career77's visibility rules.

Example:

Job Status: Published
Visibility: Public

Candidates can:

View Job
Save Job
Apply
18. Pause Job

Recruiters can temporarily pause a Published Job.

Published
    ↓
Pause
    ↓
Paused

A paused Job should not accept new applications.

Existing applications should remain available to the Recruiter.

19. Resume Job

A paused Job can be made active again.

Paused
    ↓
Resume
    ↓
Published

The Job becomes available according to the platform's publishing rules.

20. Close Job

Recruiters can close a Job when recruitment is complete.

Published
    ↓
Close Job
    ↓
Confirmation
    ↓
Closed

A Closed Job should not accept new applications.

Existing applications and hiring records should remain accessible.

21. Delete Draft

Recruiters can delete Jobs that are still in Draft status.

Draft
    ↓
Delete
    ↓
Confirmation
    ↓
Deleted

Published or active Jobs should not be permanently deleted without following Career77's data and audit policies.

    ↓

25. Empty State

If the Recruiter has no Jobs:

You haven't created any jobs yet.

Create your first job opening to start hiring.

Action:

[ Create Job ]
26. Loading State

While Jobs are loading:

Jobs
    ↓
Loading
    ↓
Skeleton Job Cards / Table
    ↓
Jobs Loaded

The layout should remain stable during loading.

27. Error State

If Jobs cannot be loaded:

Unable to load jobs.

Please try again.

Action:

[ Retry ]

Internal errors must not be displayed to Recruiters.

28. Job Validation

Before publishing, the system should validate required information.

Validation may include:

Job Title
Job Description
Employment Type
Experience Level
Location / Work Mode
Required Skills

Example:

Job Title is required.
Job Description is required.

The Recruiter should be able to correct validation errors before publishing.

29. Job Security

The system must:

Verify Recruiter authentication.
Verify Recruiter role.
Verify Company ownership.
Prevent unauthorized Job modification.
Protect Applicant information.
Validate Job data.
Enforce backend authorization.
Record important Job actions.

Recruiters must only manage Jobs belonging to Companies they are authorized to manage.

31. Job Acceptance Criteria

The Recruiter Jobs feature is complete when:

Recruiters can view Jobs.
Recruiters can create Jobs.
Recruiters can save Jobs as Draft.
Recruiters can edit Jobs.
Recruiters can review Job details.
Recruiters can publish Jobs.
Recruiters can pause Jobs.
Recruiters can resume Jobs.
Recruiters can close Jobs.
Recruiters can delete Draft Jobs.
Recruiters can view Applicants for each Job.
Recruiters can search Jobs.
Recruiters can filter Jobs.
Recruiters can sort Jobs.
Job validation is implemented.
Job status is clearly displayed.
Empty states are handled.
Loading states are handled.
Error states are handled.
Job ownership is verified.
Backend authorization is enforced.
Jobs are responsive.
Jobs are accessible.
