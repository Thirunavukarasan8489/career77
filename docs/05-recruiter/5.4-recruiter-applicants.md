1. Purpose

The Recruiter Applicants feature allows Recruiters to view, search, filter, and manage Candidates who have applied for their Jobs.

Recruiters can:

View Applicants
Search Applicants
Filter Applicants
View Candidate Profiles
View Resumes
Review Applications
Shortlist Candidates
Reject Candidates
Move Candidates to Hiring Pipeline
Schedule Interviews
2. Applicants Access

Route:

/recruiter/applicants

Access requires:

Authenticated
+
Role = Recruiter
+
Account Status = Active
+
Verification Status = Approved

14. Shortlist Candidate

Recruiters can shortlist suitable Candidates.

Flow:

Applicant
    ↓
Review
    ↓
Shortlist
    ↓
Confirmation
    ↓
Status = Shortlisted

Example:

[ Shortlist Candidate ]

After shortlisting, the Candidate may receive a notification.

15. Reject Candidate

Recruiters can reject Candidates who are not selected for the next stage.

Flow:

Applicant
    ↓
Review
    ↓
Reject
    ↓
Confirmation
    ↓
Status = Rejected

The Recruiter may optionally provide a rejection reason.

Example:

Reason:
Experience does not match current requirements.

The rejection reason should only be visible to the Candidate if Career77's notification policy allows it.

16. Move to Hiring Pipeline

Recruiters can move Applicants into the Hiring Pipeline.

Applicant
    ↓
Shortlist
    ↓
Hiring Pipeline

Example:

Applied
    ↓
Screening
    ↓
Shortlisted

The Applicant should remain connected to the original Job and Application.

18. Applicant Bulk Actions

Recruiters may perform bulk actions where supported.

Example:

Select Applicants

[ Shortlist Selected ]
[ Reject Selected ]
[ Move to Pipeline ]

Bulk actions must require confirmation before execution.

The system should record important bulk actions in Audit Logs.

    ↓

23. Error State

If Applicants cannot be loaded:

Unable to load applicants.

Please try again.

Action:

[ Retry ]

Internal API or database errors must not be exposed.

24. Applicant Data Security

Recruiters must only access Applicants associated with Jobs they are authorized to manage.

Example:

Recruiter A
    ↓
Company A
    ↓
Company A Jobs
    ↓
Company A Applicants

Recruiter A must not access:

Company B Applicants

The backend must verify:

Recruiter
    ↓
Company Ownership / Membership
    ↓
Job Ownership
    ↓
Applicant Access
25. Candidate Privacy

The Recruiter Applicants feature must respect Candidate privacy settings.

Recruiters should only access information that:

Is required for recruitment.
Is shared by the Candidate.
Is permitted by Candidate privacy settings.
Is authorized by Career77 policies.

Sensitive Candidate information must be protected.
