1. Purpose

The Recruiter Interviews feature allows Recruiters to schedule, manage, and track Candidate Interviews throughout the hiring process.

Recruiters can:

View Interviews
Schedule Interviews
Reschedule Interviews
Cancel Interviews
Manage Interview Details
Add Interviewers
Add Interview Notes
Track Interview Status
View Interview History
2. Interviews Access

Route:

/recruiter/interviews

Access requires:

Authenticated
+
Role = Recruiter
+
Account Status = Active
+
Verification Status = Approved

32. Interview Data Security

Recruiters must only access Interviews related to:

Their Company
    ↓
Their Authorized Jobs
    ↓
Their Authorized Candidates

Interview feedback must be restricted to authorized users.

Meeting links and private Interview information must be protected.
