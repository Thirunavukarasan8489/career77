# Career77 — Responsive Design: Saved Jobs

## Mobile Viewport (<640px)
*   The Saved Job list renders as full-width cards instead of grids.
*   Action buttons expand to tap target heights of at least `48px`.
*   Grid margins adjust down to `12px` to maximize space.

## Tablet & Desktop (>=640px)
*   Jobs render as standard multi-column grid items.
*   The "Save Job" button is placed at the top-right corner of each job card.
