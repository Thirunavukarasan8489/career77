# Functional Requirements — Saved Jobs

## FR-001: Save Action
*   **Requirement**: Candidates must be able to toggle the save/bookmark status of an active job opening.
*   **User**: Candidate.
*   **Preconditions**: Candidate session active.
*   **Action**: Clicks the "Save" icon on a job card.
*   **Expected Result**: Job is added to the candidate's saved list.

---

## FR-002: Saved Jobs Dashboard
*   **Requirement**: Candidates must have a dedicated view listing their saved jobs.
*   **User**: Candidate.
*   **Preconditions**: Candidate session active.
*   **Action**: Navigates to `/candidate/saved`.
*   **Expected Result**: Renders a list of saved jobs with titles, companies, locations, and quick actions to view or unsave.
