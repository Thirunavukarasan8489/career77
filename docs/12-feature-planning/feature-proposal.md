# Career77 — Feature Proposal

## Feature Name
Candidate Saved Jobs

## Feature Category
Candidate

## Primary Role
Candidate

## Secondary Roles
None

## Problem Statement
As a candidate browsing jobs on Career77, I have difficulty keeping track of interesting jobs that I want to apply to later because there is no way to bookmark or save them. This causes me to repeatedly search for the same jobs, leading to frustration and potential loss of applications.

## Who Has This Problem?
Active job-seeking candidates.

## Current User Experience
Candidates browse openings, but if they want to review a job later, they must bookmark the URL externally or search for it again.

## Proposed User Experience
Add a "Save Job" button on the job listing cards and detail pages, allowing candidates to view a curated list of bookmarked jobs on their dashboard.

## Why This Feature Is Needed
Improves candidate retention and increases the conversion rate from view to application.

## Business Value
Higher candidate engagement metrics and application volume.

## User Value
Reduces the friction of managing job searches.

## Expected Outcome
Candidates can easily bookmark and track jobs.

## Success Metrics
*   Saved Job Click Rate (Saves per user)
*   Saved-to-Applied Conversion Rate

## Dependencies
*   Candidate Session API

## Risks
*   Database index bloat if unchecked, mitigated by limiting saves to 50 active jobs per candidate.

## Out of Scope
*   Recruiter alerts for who saved their jobs.

## Priority
P2

## Estimated Complexity
Low

## Recommendation
Approve proposal to move to planning.
