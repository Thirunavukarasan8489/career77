# Career77 — Acceptance Criteria: Saved Jobs

## AC-001: Save Job Toggle
*   **Given** a logged-in Candidate on the Job Details page
*   **When** they click the "Save Job" button
*   **Then** the button text changes to "Saved"
*   **And** the job ID is appended to the Candidate's `savedJobs` array.

## AC-002: Dashboard Visibility
*   **Given** a logged-in Candidate on their dashboard
*   **When** they open the "Saved Jobs" tab
*   **Then** they see all active and closed bookmarked jobs.
