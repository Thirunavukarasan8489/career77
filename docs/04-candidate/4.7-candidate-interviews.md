1. Purpose

The Candidate Interviews feature allows Candidates to view and manage interviews related to their job applications.

Candidates can:

View Upcoming Interviews
View Interview Details
Join Online Interviews
Track Interview Status
View Past Interviews
Receive Interview Notifications
2. Interview Access

Route:

/candidate/interviews

Access requires:

Authenticated
+
Role = Candidate
+
Account Status = Active
3. Interview Flow
Application
    ↓
Candidate Shortlisted
    ↓
Interview Scheduled
    ↓
Candidate Notification
    ↓
View Interview Details
    ↓
Attend Interview
    ↓
Interview Completed
    ↓
Hiring Decision
4. Interview Dashboard

The Interview page should display:

Interviews
│
├── Upcoming
├── Today
├── Completed
└── Cancelled

Candidates should be able to quickly identify their next interview.

Display upcoming interview information.

    ↓

11. Interview Confirmation

If Candidate confirmation is required:

Interview Invitation
    ↓
View Details
    ↓
Confirm Attendance

Actions:

[ Confirm ]
[ Request Reschedule ]

The Candidate should receive confirmation after successfully responding.

12. Reschedule Request

Candidates may request an interview reschedule if enabled.

Flow:

Interview
    ↓
Request Reschedule
    ↓
Select / Enter Preferred Time
    ↓
Add Reason
    ↓
Submit Request
    ↓
Recruiter Review

The Recruiter must approve or reject the request.

The interview remains unchanged until the Recruiter confirms the new schedule.

13. Interview Cancellation

If an interview is cancelled:

Scheduled Interview
    ↓
Cancelled
    ↓
Candidate Notification

The Candidate should see:

Interview Cancelled

The cancellation reason may be displayed if provided.

14. Interview Notifications

Candidates should receive notifications for:

New Interview Scheduled
Interview Confirmed
Interview Rescheduled
Interview Cancelled
Interview Reminder
Interview Completed

Notifications may be delivered through:

In-App Notification
Email
15. Interview Reminders

The system may send reminders before an interview.

Example:

1 Day Before
1 Hour Before

The exact reminder schedule should be configurable.

16. Past Interviews

Candidates can view completed interviews.

Example:

25 July 2026

Past interview information should remain available through the Candidate's application history.

17. Empty State

If there are no upcoming interviews:

No upcoming interviews.

When a Recruiter schedules an interview,
it will appear here.

Optional action:

[ Search Jobs ]
18. Loading State

While interviews are loading:

Interviews
    ↓
Loading
    ↓
Skeleton Interview Cards
    ↓
Interview Data

The layout should remain stable during loading.

19. Error State

If interviews cannot be loaded:

Unable to load your interviews.

Please try again.

Action:

[ Retry ]

Internal system errors must not be displayed.

22. Interview Security

The system must:

Require Candidate authentication.
Verify Candidate ownership.
Protect meeting links.
Protect interview information.
Prevent unauthorized access.
Enforce backend authorization.

A Candidate must only see interviews associated with their own applications.

24. Interview Acceptance Criteria

The Interviews feature is complete when:

Candidate can view upcoming interviews.
Candidate can view interview details.
Candidate can view past interviews.
Candidate can join online interviews.
Candidate can confirm interviews where required.
Candidate can request rescheduling where allowed.
Candidate receives interview notifications.
Interview reminders are supported.
Interview status is displayed.
Cancelled interviews are clearly identified.
Calendar view is available where implemented.
Empty states are handled.
Loading states are handled.
Error states are handled.
Interview information is protected.
Meeting links are protected.
Backend authorization is enforced.
Interview pages are responsive.
Interview pages are accessible.
