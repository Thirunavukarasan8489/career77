1. Purpose

This document defines the subscription architecture for Career77.

Subscriptions are primarily used for recruiter and company paid plans.

Candidates may access free platform features and separately purchase paid training.

2. Subscription Users

    ↓

Super Admin manages subscription plans and platform-level subscription operations.

29. Subscription Deactivation

A plan should not necessarily be deleted when it is no longer available.

Instead:

Plan
    ↓
isActive = false

Existing subscribers may continue under the applicable business rules.

New customers cannot select the inactive plan.

30. Subscription Security

Career77 should:

Verify subscription status server-side
Validate payment webhooks
Prevent unauthorized subscription changes
Protect billing credentials
Check company ownership
Prevent duplicate subscription activation
Audit important subscription actions

34. Final Subscription Principle

Career77 subscriptions should be:

Flexible
    ↓
Secure
    ↓
Configurable
    ↓
Payment-Verified
    ↓
Usage-Aware
    ↓
Auditable

Subscription status and feature access must always be verified server-side.

The system should separate plans, subscriptions, orders, payments, and usage limits to maintain a clean and scalable architecture.
