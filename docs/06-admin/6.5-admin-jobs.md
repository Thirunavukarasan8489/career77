1. Purpose

The Admin Jobs feature allows Super Admins to monitor and manage Jobs posted on Career77.

Super Admins can:

View Jobs
Search Jobs
Filter Jobs
Review Job Details
Approve Jobs
Reject Jobs
Request Changes
Suspend Jobs
Close Jobs
Restore Eligible Jobs
2. Jobs Access

Route:

/admin/jobs

Access requires:

Authenticated
+
Role = Super Admin
+
Account Status = Active
3. Job Management Flow
Super Admin
    ↓
Jobs
    ↓
Search / Filter Jobs
    ↓
Select Job
    ↓
View Job Details
    ↓
Review Job
    ↓
Take Action
    │
    ├── Approve
    ├── Request Changes
    ├── Reject
    ├── Suspend
    ├── Close
    └── Restore

18. Suspend Job

    ↓

Super Admin Review
    ↓
Suspend

    ↓
Confirm
    ↓
Status = Suspended

A suspended Job should no longer appear in normal Candidate Job Search results.

Super Admins may close a Job when required.

    ↓
