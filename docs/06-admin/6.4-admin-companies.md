1. Purpose

The Admin Companies feature allows Super Admins to manage and monitor Companies registered on Career77.

Super Admins can:

View Companies
Search Companies
Filter Companies
Review Company Details
Verify Companies
Reject Companies
Request Changes
Suspend Companies
Activate Companies
2. Companies Access

Route:

/admin/companies

Access requires:

Authenticated
+
Role = Super Admin
+
Account Status = Active
3. Company Management Flow
Super Admin
    ↓
Companies
    ↓
Search / Filter Companies
    ↓
Select Company
    ↓
View Company Details
    ↓
Review Company
    ↓
Take Action
    │
    ├── Approve / Verify
    ├── Request Changes
    ├── Reject
    ├── Suspend
    └── Activate

    ↓
